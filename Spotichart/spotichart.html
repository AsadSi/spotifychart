Top Songs Global

Glass Animals' “Heat Waves” has hit #1 on the Billboard Hot 100. The track now holds the record for the longest climb to the top spot — it has logged 59 weeks on the chart. “Heat Waves” came out as a single in June 2020, a couple months prior to the release of Dreamland, the album it's featured on. Its first week on the Billboard Hot 100 was on the chart dated January 16, 2021; it went off for two weeks and then returned and has been gradually climbing up ever since.
